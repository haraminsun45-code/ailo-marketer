## Instagram

### Slide 1: Drivers should record less

Freight work already requires driving, loading, unloading, and communication.

### Slide 2: Manual logs create friction

Work logs, consignment checks, calls, and photos often force repeated screen interaction.

### Slide 3: AILO follows the workflow

Clock-in, movement, loading, unloading, and completion can become one operational timeline.

### Slide 4: Confirm only what matters

Cargo verification, OCR uncertainty, and destination mismatch should still ask for driver confirmation.

### Slide 5: Driving stays first

AILO handles repetitive workflow records so drivers can focus on the road and cargo.

### Caption

Freight operations still rely on repetitive manual records. AILO is building a hands-free workflow assistant that automates low-risk logs and asks for confirmation only when operational verification matters.
